# Mixing & Mastering Showcase

## Overview
This document presents selected projects where I worked as mixing and mastering engineer, with a strong focus on vocal clarity, tone and emotional communication.

## Scope of Work
- Full mix and master of complete music multitracks 
- Detailed vocal treatment and balance  
- Dynamic control and tonal shaping  
- Spatial design and final loudness optimization
